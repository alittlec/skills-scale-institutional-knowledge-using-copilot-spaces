# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers ensure user-centric design and usability across features. They participate early in planning to align designs with business needs and technical constraints, validate features through usability testing, and support quality reviews.

### Responsibilities
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Conduct usability testing and user research
- Collaborate with Product Managers on requirements and feature specs
- Work with Developers to ensure design fidelity during implementation
- Support QA with usability acceptance criteria
- Maintain design system consistency

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support task completion
- Ensure design consistency across product surfaces

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critiques
- Usability test findings and reports
- Collaboration sessions with Product and Engineering

### Interactions with Existing Roles
Works closely with Product Manager on requirements, with Developers on implementation, and with QA for usability acceptance.

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts gather and analyze data to inform product and project decisions. They design key metrics, dashboards, and reporting methods for ongoing measurement, and collaborate with PMs and Engineering to identify opportunities from usage data.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key performance indicators (KPIs) and success metrics
- Build dashboards and reports for project and product health
- Analyze usage data to identify trends, opportunities, and risks
- Support post-release impact analysis
- Collaborate with Product and Project Managers on data-driven decisions

### Goals
- Enable data-informed decision-making across projects
- Provide timely, accurate insights to stakeholders
- Measure and communicate the impact of releases and changes

### Typical Communication
- Dashboard reviews and metric briefings
- Ad-hoc analysis reports
- Data-informed recommendations in planning and retrospectives

### Interactions with Existing Roles
Provides insights to Product and Project Managers, supports post-release analysis for continuous improvement.

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers own infrastructure setup, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment processes. They ensure system reliability, monitor for performance issues, and lead incident response when needed.

### Responsibilities
- Design, build, and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure, environments, and deployment tooling
- Monitor system health, performance, and availability
- Lead incident response and coordinate rollbacks
- Collaborate with Developers on environment and tooling needs
- Support QA with test automation infrastructure

### Goals
- Maximize deployment reliability and speed
- Minimize downtime and mean time to recovery (MTTR)
- Ensure infrastructure scales with project needs

### Typical Communication
- Deployment status and incident updates
- Infrastructure and pipeline change notifications
- Collaboration with Developers and QA on environment readiness

### Interactions with Existing Roles
Works closely with Developers for environment needs, with QA for test automation, and with Project Manager on release readiness.

---

## Customer Success Manager

### Role Summary
Customer Success Managers act as the voice of the customer, gathering feedback post-release and ensuring documented learnings are captured and actioned. They facilitate communication between end-users and the project team.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and relay customer feedback to Product and Project Managers
- Advocate for customer needs during planning and prioritization
- Support user acceptance testing and validation
- Document customer-facing learnings and improvement suggestions
- Keep stakeholders informed on customer sentiment and adoption

### Goals
- Ensure customer satisfaction and successful adoption of releases
- Close the feedback loop between users and the delivery team
- Reduce churn and increase product engagement

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries and reports
- Participation in retrospectives with customer insights
- Coordination with support and stakeholder teams

### Interactions with Existing Roles
Relays user feedback to Product and Project Managers, partners with QA to capture user acceptance, and keeps stakeholders informed.

## QA / Test Engineer

### Role Summary
QA/Test Engineers validate that deliverables meet acceptance criteria and quality standards. They design test strategies, execute tests, and work with the team to prevent and catch defects early. (This role is referenced in other process docs like the overview and execution-and-tracking but was not fully defined here.)

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain test plans, test cases, and test suites
- Execute manual and automated tests (unit, integration, end-to-end)
- Validate acceptance criteria and Definition of Done
- Report and track defects
- Collaborate with Developers on testability and with DevOps on test automation infrastructure

### Goals
- Ensure high-quality, reliable releases
- Catch defects early in the development lifecycle
- Maintain and improve test coverage

### Typical Communication
- Test status and coverage reports
- Defect triage and resolution discussions
- Collaboration with Developers and DevOps on quality tooling

## Stakeholder / Executive Sponsor

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Executive Sponsors provide strategic direction, approvals, and resources for projects. They ensure alignment between project outcomes and business objectives. (This role is referenced in multiple process docs but was not fully defined here.)

### Responsibilities
- Provide business context and strategic priorities
- Approve project charters, scope changes, and go/no-go decisions
- Remove organizational blockers and provide resources
- Participate in milestone reviews and escalation decisions
- Champion the project across the organization

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver measurable business value
- Maintain organizational alignment and resource availability
- Support teams in removing impediments

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder updates and milestone reviews
- Escalation and decision-making meetings
- Executive briefings and steering committee participation
